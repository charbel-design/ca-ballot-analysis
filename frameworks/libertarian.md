### Expected picks

A well-written libertarian framework applied to CA ballots should reliably produce:
- Skepticism of both major parties
- YES on measures reducing government scope
- NO on most new taxes
- NO on new regulatory burdens
- YES on criminal justice reforms reducing incarceration
- Support for candidates with documented civil liberties records

Note: Libertarian framework often produces more abstentions than other frameworks, because most candidates don't meet libertarian standards on civil liberties OR economic liberty. Pure-signal votes for Libertarian Party candidates in races where they appear are common.
