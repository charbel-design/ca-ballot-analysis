### Expected picks

A well-written progressive framework applied to CA ballots should reliably produce:
- Candidates with civil rights / organizing backgrounds for AG-type offices
- Candidates endorsed by labor councils for most offices
- Public-health-oriented candidates for health-adjacent offices
- YES on progressive taxation measures
- YES on tenant protections
- YES on environmental protections

If your progressive framework doesn't produce this mix, either it's a different framework (centrist-Democratic, for instance, is not the same thing) or it's producing unusual results that should be examined.
